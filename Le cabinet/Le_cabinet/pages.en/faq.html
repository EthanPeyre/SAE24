Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment in several ways:

  • By phone: +33672815638
  • Via our contact form
  • By visiting the clinic directly

Our paramedical secretary will be happy to welcome you and find a time slot that works for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash
  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Bank transfers
  • Third-party payers (insurance and mutual insurance)

Feel free to contact us for more details.

Do I need a prescription to consult?

It depends on the type of consultation:

  • General Practice: No prescription required
  • Dentistry: No prescription required
  • Physiotherapy: A prescription is recommended but not mandatory

However, a prescription can facilitate reimbursement by your insurance.

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

To cancel or reschedule an appointment:

  • Call us at +33672815638
  • Email us at gemantique@contact.fr
  • Important: Notify us at least 24 hours before your appointment

This allows us to offer your time slot to other patients.

Do you accept insurance and mutual insurance?

Yes, we work with most insurance and mutual insurance providers. We notably accept:

  • CPAM (Social Security)
  • Private mutual insurance
  • Other social insurance

Feel free to contact us to verify that your insurance is accepted.

Do you offer online consultations?

To date, all our consultations are done in person to ensure the best quality of care. We believe it is important to have direct contact with our patients.

However, feel free to contact us to discuss specific situations.

What is the average waiting time?

Generally, the waiting time is short:

  • General Practice: 15-30 minutes after your appointment time
  • Physiotherapy: 10-20 minutes
  • Dentistry: 10-15 minutes

We always strive to respect your schedule.

What should I do in case of an emergency?

In case of a medical emergency:

  • Call 15 (SAMU) for a medical emergency
  • Call 112 (European emergency number)
  • Call 17 (Police) in case of assault

For less urgent dental or physiotherapy emergencies, you can call us and we will try to see you quickly.

Do you have parking spaces?

Yes, we have 6 free parking spaces in front of the clinic. Parking is accessible and free for all our patients.

If all spaces are occupied, you can easily use public parking nearby.

Is the clinic accessible for people with reduced mobility?

Yes, our clinic is 100% accessible for people with reduced mobility:

  • Adapted toilets
  • Adapted parking
  • Accessible entrance

Feel free to let us know if you have any specific needs.
