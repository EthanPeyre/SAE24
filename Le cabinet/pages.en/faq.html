How to schedule an appointment ?

You can schedule an appointment with several procedures :

  • By calling : +33 (0) 672815638
  • Through our contact list
  • Directly with us at the center

Our mediacl secretary will be pleased to welcome you and schedule you according to your availability

Which payment method would you prefer ?

We accept the following payment method :

  • Cash
  • Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Bank transfer
  • Third party payment (insurance or Mutual)

Contact us for more information.

Do you need any prescription to consult ?

This depends on your consultation :

  • General practice : No prescription asked
  • Dental care : No prescription asked
  • Phisiotherapy : A prescription is recommended but not mandatory

However, a prescription can ease the insurance process for a refund.

How to cancel or reschedule an appointment ?

To cancel or reschedule :

  • Call our number : +33 (0) 672815638
  • E-mail us at contact@cabinetpara.fr
  • Important : Warn us at least 24h before your appointment reguarding any changes

This will allow us to free a slot for other patients.

Do you accept insurance and Mutual ?

Yes, we work around and with most of them. We accept notely :

  • CPAM (Assurance Maladie)
  • Private Mutual
  • Other social insurances

Don'thesitate to contact us to check if your insurance can be accepted.

Do you provide any online appointment ?

As of today, any and every of our services are provided in person to guarantee a better assessment for your care. We think important to keep contact with our patients .

Nevertheless, don't hold up any of your requests for specific services.

What is the average wait time ?

Generally, the average delay is quite short :

  • General practice : 15-30 min after your appointed scheduled slot
  • Phisiotherapy : 10-20 min
  • Dentistry : 10-15 min

We would like to add that we are trying our best to respect your appointed time slot.

What to do in case of an emergency ?

During an urgent situation you must :

  • Call 15 (SAMU) for a medical emergency
  • Call 112 (europeen emergency number)
  • Call 17 (Police) for any type of harassment

For less urgent dental or phisical care , vous pouvez nous appeler et nous essayerons de vous recevoir rapidement.

Do you have any parking spot ?

Yes, we have about 5 free parking spot in front of the building. Those are free and reserved for our clients.

If there isn't any spot left, you will be able to park in a nearby public parking.
